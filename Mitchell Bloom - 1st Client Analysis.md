## The Offer in Simple Words

Mitchell helps business owners and real estate investors **legally avoid (defer) the giant capital gains tax bill** they'd normally owe when they sell a business, property, or big stock position.

The core tool is a **Deferred Sales Trust (DST)** — a "seller carry-back" structure. Instead of selling directly (and getting taxed 30–45% at closing), the seller transfers the asset into a dedicated trust, the trust sells it to the buyer, and the seller gets paid over time through an installment contract. Capital gains tax is only paid gradually as payments are received — so the seller keeps their **full pre-tax proceeds working and earning interest** instead of handing a chunk to the IRS on day one.

**The one-line hook:** *"You're selling something you spent a lifetime building — you don't have to lose 30–45% of it to capital gains tax at the close. There's a legal, IRS-compliant way to defer it, keep your money working, and leave a legacy."*

**Critical timing constraint:** The strategy **must be set up BEFORE the sale closes.** Once the deal closes, the window is gone forever. This creates natural urgency and defines the ideal prospect: someone ~90 days or less from a close.

**Founder story (strong personal angle for copy):** Mitchell's father lost ~$4M in taxes when he sold Orange Julius to Dairy Queen. Watching that loss became his life's mission — to keep sellers from giving their life's work to the IRS. 37+ years of financial leadership.
