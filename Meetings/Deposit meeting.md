## The Offer / Niche

- New website: **taxfreeplan.com** (also referenced mitchbloom.com).
- Core product: a **Deferred Sales Trust (DST)** — a third-party trust takes assignment of
  an appreciated asset before closing, sells it (so the seller avoids constructive receipt
  and capital gains tax), and issues an installment note paying the seller over 10–20 years.
  Lets sellers defer capital gains and put that tax money to work.
- Cited **Rob Lowe** using a DST to sell his Montecito home as a proof point.
- Key insight: **CPAs generally don't know about this trust**, so it rarely surfaces before
  a deal closes.

### Target Client (ICP)
- **Women** selling a highly appreciated business, commercial real estate, or closely held stock.
- Deal timing: **2 weeks to 2 years out** (ideally 2 months+).
- Deal size: **$2M–$20M**, sweet spot **$5M–$50M**. $5M+ = qualified investor (easier for Mitchell).
- Other possible signals discussed: high-net-worth women getting divorced, receiving an
  inheritance, or who recently lost a spouse (Jay noted each is a different data source).
